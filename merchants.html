Self-loading

Load the sale items available in your store that you most want to share with our users. Watch your merchandise disappear.

Branding

We allow you to upload your brand as your store's avatar, tell our users about your shop, and choose the photos that go into our system.

Inventory

This part is important. When a user wants to purchase the product, we'll send you an email to make sure it is still in stock. When you confirm, the sale is settled. You need to hang on to the item until the customer picks it up or ship the item to the address we provide. All refunds are handled according to your store policy.

Account Management

While users can upload any sales from your store, the buy button will only be displayed if you upload the item from a verified merchant account.

Processing

When a customer buys your sale product through us, we handle the processing fee for a limited time. This is our way of thanking the merchants who partner with us early.

Pickup/Delivery

It's okay if you don't do delivery. Our customers will pick it up in store, but it's a big plus for them if you do. Just let us know if you offer delivery when you upload the product. We'll charge the customer a flat fee of $5 for delivery anywhere in the Continental U.S. and pass that onto you for shipping costs.
