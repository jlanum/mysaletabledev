Shopper FAQs
How are sale products sold on MySaleTable?
Once you select BUY, availability is confirmed and payment is settled.
You'll then receive an email confirming your purchase.
Depending on your delivery selection, the item is shipped or you pick it up at the store.
Can products be shipped?
Yes, if the store offers shipping. You can see if shipping is available in the product detail box.
Are products returnable?
Each store determines if a product is returnable.
This information is provided in the product detail box.
If the store allows returns, you have (10) days from purchase to return your purchase.
How do I return a purchase?
If the retailer accepts returns, please contact them directly.
If you have questions, please send us an email: hi@mysaletable.com.
What happens if a product is not available?
Your credit card will not be charged and you will receive an email alert telling you so.
If you have any questions, send us an email: hi@mysaletable.com
Is it free to shop on MySaleTable?
Yes. And our iPhone app is also free!
Who determines the retail and sale prices for a product?
Sale items sold on MySaleTable are priced by the stores that post them.
How much time do I have to pick-up my purchase at the store?
If you're picking up your purchase in-store, we ask that you claim it within one week.
However, your purchase will be held until you get there. Go ahead and call the store if you need more time to get there!
Can I use PayPal?
Not at this time. However, we do accept VISA, MasterCard and American Express credit cards.
Can I shop by phone?
All purchases must be made through our website or our mobile app. We keep it quick and simple!
How do I share my favorite a shop with you?
We love recommendations! Send us an email, a Tweet (Twitter), and a post (Facebook).
Contact us any way you want!
