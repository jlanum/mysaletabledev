Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase products.
Click the buy button from your device or laptop. Follow the prompts. Once the merchant confirms the sale, we will settle the payment on the card you provided. The will be held for you at the store specified.
When do I get my confirmation email?
You will be notified regarding availability within 24 hours.
Can I return the item if I want?
All returns are handled by the merchants. Users are subject to the return policies of the store from which the goods are purchased.
How long will the goods be held at the store?
While speed is encouraged, the item should be held until you pick it up.
What happens if the item is out of stock?
This shouldn't occur; however, if the merchant misrepresents availability, your card will not be charged. You will receive an explanatory email.
What if I don't see my sizes?
We can only ensure availability of the sizes listed.
Is there a charge for purchasing through the app or website?
No, MySaleTable does not tack on any additional charges.
Can other shoppers buy the products I post on the site?
No, in order to ensure product availability and price accuracy, we only allow verified merchants' posts to include a buy flow.
