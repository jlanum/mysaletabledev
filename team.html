Cofounder

Patricia Calfee is an ecommerce and community entrepreneur. Previously, she co-founded Bergine, a local flash-sale business acquired by Gilt Groupe in 2010. Then, while expecting her first baby, she built Roses & Rye, a specialty footwear business celebrated by People, WWD, and BabyCenter.

Before becoming a parent, she created CSG, a non-profit education program that serves more than 13,000 K-8 students online and in the classroom. In 2006, CSG was acquired by the San Francisco Unified School District. Patricia's work was honored with the AT&T Community Hero Award and AIGA Cause/Effect Award.

She began her career managing public relations and marketing campaigns for Ian Schrager Company, Young & Rubicam, and Razorfish. Patricia earned a BA in Economics from the University of San Francisco.

Cofounder

Ian Picache is an investor, entrepreneur, and Dad. Since 1997, he has invested more than $300MM in businesses spanning a wide range of industries, including Internet, ecommerce, and telecommunications.

Ian previously invested with Technology Crossover Ventures, Vanguard Ventures, and Telesoft Partners, and currently through Picache Holdings. He began his career at Morgan Stanley.

In 2010, he co-founded Bergine, a luxury local flash-sale business. Seven months after launching, Internet retail pioneer Gilt Groupe acquired the business. Ian holds a BA in Economics and Engineering from Dartmouth College and MBA from The Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
