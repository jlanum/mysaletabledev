Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a merchant account?
Go to the merchant page and read through the information. From there, click on the merchant page and follow the prompts.
How do I update inventory?
As a merchant user, you have permission to update the product. When it has been sold, you can delete the sale.
How do I know when my item has been sold?
You will receive an email from shop@mysaletable.com with the item details. Confirm that you are holding the product, and we will process the sale.
How long do I have to respond to the email?
The sooner you can respond, the better. We will hold the sale for up to [X] days. After that, we will cancel the sale.
What happens if the item is out of stock?
We ask that you keep track of your inventory on MySaleTable to avoid this situation; however, if a shopper purchases a sale item through the site that is no longer in stock, we ask that you inform us via email. We will delete the sale and explain the situation to the shopper.
How do I load multiple sizes?
For now, we are asking that you load each sale item individually. We are working on a solution that allows for higher volume uploads.
What do you charge for posting?
There is no charge for posting sale items on MySaleTable! During the pilot program, we will not take a percentage of the sale and will cover the cost of running the shopper's card. Thanks for joining early!
Can I use my sale pics in my newsletter?
Yes, we encourage you to use MySaleTable posts in your customer relations. We are working on making this even easier for you.
