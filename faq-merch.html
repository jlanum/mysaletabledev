Retailer FAQs

What is MySaleTable?

MySaleTable is an easy way for stores to sell their (fabulous) sale items that otherwise sit on tables and racks as the season ticks by.

Our retail partners connect with new shoppers -- online and with our mobile app -- while selling their sale inventory.

The MySaleTable marketplace allows users to shop with confidence. We directly handle all transactions, ensuring that our community members are happy. We stand behind every purchase and are excited to work with stores who share our value of kind and generous customer service value.

    • What are the fees for selling on MySaleTable?

    We keep it simple: it is free to post a sale item on MySaleTable. To show appreciation for our launch retail partners we are guaranteeing the lifetime commission fee of a 10%. This means that you will always retain 90% of your sale as earnings. In the future, this rate will change.

    Once your item has shipped, your earnings are electronically transferred to your account.
    How do I know when my item has sold?
    When an item has sold, you'll receive an email from MySaleTable asking you to quickly confirm that the product is available.
    Simply reply and we'll process the sale for you! You will then receive a confirmation email and be alerted regarding delivery (shipping or pick-up).
    How do I update my post?
    Simply tag an item as "sold" if it's no longer available.
    What if there are multiple sizes of a product available?
    Select as many sizes as are available when uploading your post.
    Shoppers will select their size when purchasing.
    My item just sold - what do I do!?

    Congratulations! This is exciting.

    We provide shipping for you (up to 5 lbs.). If you've opted to allow shipping, go ahead and generate a shipping label using the information we've sent via email. If the item is being picked up, your customer should be in store within (4) days from your email reply.

    Have questions? Send us an email (we reply quickly): stores@mysaletable.com.
    How am I paid for my sale?
    When your sale is confirmed, we send the funds directly to your bank account within (7) days. We keep things simple!
    How does MySaleTable handle returns?
    Each retailer determines if they'll accept a return on a item when they post the sale.
    Is there a minimum sale percentage for sales?
    A sale item should be at least 25% off your retail price.
    Can I sell vintage/antique/used items on MySaleTable?
    MySaleTable is a marketplace for new sale products from stores and shops across America.
    Please do not post vintage, antique or used items. Thank you! :)
    I don't have an iPhone, can I still sell on MySaleTable?
    Yes! If you don't have an iPhone, simply use the upload function on our website to load photos and product details of your sale. Our drag-n-drop function for photo upload makes it very easy.
