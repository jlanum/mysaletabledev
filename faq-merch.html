Retailer FAQs

How can I sell my store's sale items on MySaleTable?
If you have a shop and want to sell your fabulous sale items across America, then go here and create a store account.
Once confirmed, you can post and sell products on MySaleTable.
How do I post a product for sale?
Simply upload product photos and information with our iPhone app or website.
Be sure to read our helpful Photo Tips here.
How do I know when my product has sold?
You'll receive an email from MySaleTable asking you to quickly confirm that the product is available.
Simply reply and we'll process the sale for you!
How long do I have to respond to sale alert email?
The sooner you respond, the better! We will hold the sale for up to 24 hours.
How do I update inventory?
As a retailer, you can tag a sale item as SOLD if it's no longer available.
How do I load multiple sizes?
Just select as many sizes as you have available when uploading.
The shopper will select the size they want when purchasing.
Is there a charge for posting?
There is no charge for posting sale items on MySaleTable.
When an item sells, MySaleTable receives a percentage of the sale.
We are thankful for our launch retail partners, so for a limited time, our retail partners receive a fee of 10% for life!
Can I use my sale pics in my newsletter?
Yes, feel free to share your MySaleTable posts with your customers.
We're working to make this even easier for you.
What if the size chart doesn't include the size of my product?
If size is not applicable to your item, go ahead and select N/A.
If the size is uncommon, please write a description in the comments section.
How will I be reimbursed for a purchase?
MySaleTable reimburses you electronically.
Can I sell vintage/antique/used items on MySaleTable?
MySaleTable sells only new products from stores and shops across America.
Please do not post vintage, antique or used items on the site.
Is there a minimum % off for a sale to post?
We want to excite our members, so sale items should be reduced by at least 25% off the original retail price.
